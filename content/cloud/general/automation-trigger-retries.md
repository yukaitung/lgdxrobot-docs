When a trigger fails, the system logs the error in **Trigger Retries** to allow the trigger to be re-run. The retry can be executed once or for every failed request for the same trigger.

## Retrying a Trigger

* Click the **View** button on the right of the trigger.
* Click **Retry This Trigger** to retry the trigger, or **Retry All Failed Requests** to retry all failed requests for the same trigger.
* After pressing **Retry All Failed Requests**, press **Confirm** to proceed.

## Deleting a Trigger Retry

* Click the **View** button on the right of the trigger.
* Click **Delete** to delete the trigger retry.
* Press **Delete** to confirm.
