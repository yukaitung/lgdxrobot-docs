This page has real-time capabilities.
{.alert .alert-info}

The Home page is designed to monitor the status of automation tasks and the fleet in the most convenient way. The segmented control at the top is used to switch between different views.

## Tasks

![Tasks View](../img/home/tasks.png)

The Tasks view shows the status of automation tasks.

* **Charts**: Display task statistics from the last 24 hours.
* **Running Tasks**: Shows up to 50 running tasks in a table.
* **Waiting Queue**: Shows up to 50 queued tasks in a table.

## Map

![Map View](../img/home/map.png)

The map displays the locations of online robots in the fleet. To move the map, drag it in any direction. To zoom in or out, use the mouse wheel or the +/- buttons in the top-right corner.

![Map View With Panel](../img/home/map-panel.png)

When a robot is online, it appears on the map automatically. To view the status of a robot, click on it. A side panel will appear on the right with the following information:

* **Robot Data**: The robot’s position, battery level, and status.
* **Task**: Details of the task the robot is currently executing.
* **Navigation**: The robot’s navigation data from Nav2.
* Additionally, the robot’s current paths are displayed as blue lines on the map.

## Robots

![Robots View](../img/home/robots.png)

The Robots view shows the status of every robot in the fleet. The search bar at the top can be used to filter robots by name.
