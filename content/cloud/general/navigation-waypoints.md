A *Waypoint* is a location on the Realm that a robot can navigate to during a Task. Waypoints represent goal positions used by Nav2.

## Creating a Waypoint

* Click the **Create Waypoint** button at the top right.
* Enter the waypoint settings.
* Click **Create**.

## Editing a Waypoint

* Click the **View** button on the right of the waypoint.
* Update the waypoint settings.
* Click **Update**.

## Deleting a Waypoint

* Click the **View** button on the right of the waypoint.
* Click **Delete**.
* The system will verify that no entities are referencing the waypoint.
* Click **Delete** again to confirm.

## Settings

* **Name**: The name of the waypoint.
* **X (m)**: The X coordinate of the waypoint in metres.
* **Y (m)**: The Y coordinate of the waypoint in metres.
* **Rotation (rad)**: The rotation of the waypoint in radians.
* **Facilities**: The list of facilities available at the waypoint.

The Facilities setting does not affect system behaviour at this time.
