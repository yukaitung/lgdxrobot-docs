This section provides documentation for the functionality of LGDXRobot Cloud. The functionality is divided into the following parts:

* **Navigation**: Manages the fleet, including maps, waypoints, and robots.
* **Automation**: Manages automation tasks, including progress, workflows, and steps. It also manages triggers to third-party systems.
* **Administration**: Manages system settings, including users, roles, access, and auditing.
