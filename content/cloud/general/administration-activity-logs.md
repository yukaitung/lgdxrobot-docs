Activity Logs are a record of all actions performed in the system. The logs also appear on the details page of each entity.

## Filtering Activity Logs

Activity Logs are displayed for every entity. To filter the logs, click the **Filter Entities** dropdown at the top right.

The logs can also be filtered by the ID of the entity, including a number, GUID, or email template name.
