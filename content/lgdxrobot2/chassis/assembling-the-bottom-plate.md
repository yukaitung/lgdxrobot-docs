### Controller Board 1

solder the components to the controller board, then install the modules to the board.

### Motors

Insert the screw to the bracket and the motors and ighten with a screwdriver. Repeat for the other motors.

Then install the wheels to the motors and tighten with a hex key.

Mount the motors to the lower plate with the hex key, ensuring the should form a X when looking from the top. Note all the moters with numbers accroding to the picture.

### Controller Board 2

Mount the controller board to the lower plate with the hex key. Use the nut to tighten the screws.

### Power Supplu

Conenect the Battery case with the 18650 Protection Board accroding to the picture.

Install the DC-DC Buck Converter on the plate.

Use wires to connect the components below.

### Camera

Mount the camera to the bottom plate.
