## Motors

Insert the screw to the motor bracket and the motors and tighten them with a screwdriver. The motor shaft can be facing up or down, this only changes the height of the robot. Repeat for the other motors and ensure the shaft is facing same direction.

Then install the wheels to the motors and tighten with a hex key.

A typical Mecanum wheel installation should form a X when looking from the top. the  Note all the moters with numbers accroding to the picture. The mound hole for camera indicate the forward direction.

Insert M4x12 mm screws from the top of bottom plate to the the bracket holes. Secure the motor using M4 nuts on the opposite side. Repeat for the other holes and motors and check the installation is correct. It is recommended to tag the motors with numbers on both motors and the bottom plate.

Incorrect installation could cause the robot behave abnormally.
{.alert .alert-info}

## Controller Board

Insert the M4 screws to the controller board to the bottom plate, secure the board with M4 nuts on the opposite side. Ensure the PH2.0 connector is facing to the front side.

## Power Supply

Place the both DC-DC Buck Converter around the center of the bottom plate, then place the 18650 Battery case on the both left and right side of the bottom plate. You can secure them using tape or hot glue.

The left side of the power supply is for PC, solider the wire to connect the power supply --> Power Switch --> INA226 --> DC-DC Buck Converter --> DC Power Jack / USB and plug it to the PC.

The right side of the power supply is for the motors, solider the wire to connect the power supply --> INA226 --> Relay Module (Optionally for solidering) --> Emergency Stop Button --> DC - DC Buck Converter --> XT30 Connector and plug it to the boards.

Stick / Glue both Power Switch and Emergency Stop Button to the bottom plate.

## Camera

Place the camera on the front and secure it with 1/4-Inch Tripod Screw.
