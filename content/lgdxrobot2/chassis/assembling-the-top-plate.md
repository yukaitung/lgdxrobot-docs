Place the LiDAR on the top plate, insert M2.5 screws, and tighten them with a hex key. Connect the LiDAR cable.

![Image of the install LiDAR](../img/asm_top/lidar.png)

Insert M4 standoffs on the bottom plate, then insert M4 screws and tighten them with a hex key.

![Image of the install LiDAR](../img/asm_top/standoffs.png)

Position the top plate onto the bottom plate, insert M4 screws, and tighten them with a hex key.

![Image of the install Top Plate](../img/asm_top/final.png)

## Camera

Place the camera at the front of the chassis and secure it using a 1/4-inch tripod screw.

![Image of the installation of the camera](../img/asm_bottom/camera.png)

Install the batteries for the power supply.

Warning: Ensure that the batteries are installed with the correct polarity.
