---
title: Assembling the PC Plate
weight: 6
---

The install depends on the type of PC you are using. 3 kinds of PC are supported including Nvidia Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi, and NUC.

### Nvidia Jetson Nano

Mount the M2.5 standoffs to the PC plate with the hex key according the picture, then mount the Jetson Nano to the standoffs.

### Raspberry Pi

Mount the M2.5 standoffs to the PC plate with the hex key according the picture, then mount the Raspberry Pi to the standoffs.

### NUC

Only Slim Kit is supported. Mount the M4 screws to the PC plate with the hex key according the picture, put the NUC on the plate. Insert the M3 screws on the bottom of the NUC.

### Bottom Plate

If you are not using NUC, mount the M4 screws to the PC plate with the hex key according the picture, then mount the bottom plate to the Bottom plate.

Plug the usb cables for the controller board, camera and the LiDAR. Plug the power cable to the battery.
