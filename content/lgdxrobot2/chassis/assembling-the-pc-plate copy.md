---
title: Assembling the PC Plate
weight: 7
---

The install depends on the type of PC you are using. 3 kinds of PC are supported including Nvidia Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi, and NUC.

### Nvidia Jetson Nano

Insert the M2.5 screws to the PC plate to the M2.5 standoffs, fasten with a hex key, Then insert M2.5 screws to the Jetson Nano and fasten with a hex key.

Insert M4 screws to the PC plate to the M4 standoffs, fasten with a hex key, Then insert M4 screws to the bottom plate and fasten with a hex key.

Connect the usb cables for the controller board, camera and the LiDAR.

### Raspberry Pi

Insert the M2.5 screws to the PC plate to the M2.5 standoffs, fasten with a hex key, Then insert M2.5 screws to the Raspberry Pi and fasten with a hex key.

Insert M4 screws to the PC plate to the M4 standoffs, fasten with a hex key, Then insert M4 screws to the bottom plate and fasten with a hex key.

Connect the usb cables for the controller board, camera and the LiDAR.

### NUC

Insert M4 screws to the PC plate to the M4 standoffs, fasten with a hex key, 

Then put the NUC on the PC plate and insert M3 screws on the bottom of the NUC. Fasten with a hex key.

Then insert M4 screws to the bottom plate and fasten with a hex key.

Connect the usb cables for the controller board, camera and the LiDAR.
