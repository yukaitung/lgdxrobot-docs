---
title: Assembling the PC Plate
weight: 7
---

The installation depends on the type of PC you are using. Three types of PC are supported: Nvidia Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi, and NUC.

### Nvidia Jetson Nano

1. Insert M2.5 screws through the PC plate into the M2.5 standoffs and fasten with a hex key. Then secure the Jetson Nano to the standoffs using M2.5 screws and a hex key.
2. Insert M4 screws through the PC plate into the M4 standoffs and fasten with a hex key. Then secure the PC plate to the bottom plate using M4 screws and a hex key.
3. Connect the USB cables for the controller board, camera, and LiDAR.

### Raspberry Pi

1. Insert M2.5 screws through the PC plate into the M2.5 standoffs and fasten with a hex key. Then secure the Raspberry Pi to the standoffs using M2.5 screws and a hex key.
2. Insert M4 screws through the PC plate into the M4 standoffs and fasten with a hex key. Then secure the PC plate to the bottom plate using M4 screws and a hex key.
3. Connect the USB cables for the controller board, camera, and LiDAR.

### NUC

1. Insert M4 screws through the PC plate into the M4 standoffs and fasten with a hex key.
2. Place the NUC on the PC plate and secure it using M3 screws at the bottom of the NUC, fastening with a hex key.
3. Secure the PC plate to the bottom plate using M4 screws and a hex key.
4. Connect the USB cables for the controller board, camera, and LiDAR.
